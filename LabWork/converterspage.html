Unit Conversions
Temperature



To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use the formula, replacing C with your temperature in celsius: (C x 9/5)+32
Weight



To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use the formula, replacing kg with your weight in kilograms: kg x 2.205
